Online Apps vs Desktop Apps
🖥 Desktop Apps
✅ VBA, Macros & advanced automation
✅ Full Power Query and Power Pivot
✅ Complex data models
✅ Advanced animations and designs (PowerPoint)
✅ Full offline support — no Internet required
✅ COM add-ins and Windows system integration
✅ Large files (millions of rows in Excel)
✅ Better compatibility with complex file formats
✅ Print options and advanced mail merge

✅ Real-time collaboration with colleagues
✅ Access from any device with a browser
✅ Ideal for light work and quick reviews
⚠️ No VBA / Macros support
⚠️ Limited Power Query (Excel Online)
⚠️ Advanced animations unavailable (PowerPoint Online)
❌ Always requires an active Internet connection
❌ No COM add-ins or local fonts
❌ Limited advanced design capabilities
