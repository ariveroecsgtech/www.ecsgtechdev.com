What Is RMM?

Proactive IT Care — Before Problems Happen

Remote Monitoring and Management (RMM) is the foundation of modern managed IT. It gives ECSG continuous, real-time visibility into every device, server, and service in your environment — enabling us to detect issues, apply fixes, and enforce security policies automatically, often before you ever notice a problem.

Proactive, Not Reactive

Traditional IT support waits for things to break — then sends someone to fix it. ECSG's RMM flips that model entirely. A lightweight agent installed on each device sends real-time health data to our platform, where automated rules and our engineering team watch for early warning signs: disk space approaching capacity, memory pressure, CPU spikes, failed services, missed backups, or emerging security threats.

When an issue is detected, our automated remediation scripts attempt to resolve it instantly — without human intervention. When escalation is needed, our NOC team receives an alert and acts within minutes, resolving most issues remotely before your users experience any disruption.

What RMM Includes

End-to-End Visibility & Control

Our RMM service provides comprehensive coverage of your IT environment — from automated patching and endpoint security to backup monitoring and cloud service visibility.

Continuous System Monitoring

Real-time monitoring of servers, workstations, network devices, and cloud services — detecting anomalies, errors, and early failure signs before they become outages.

  • CPU, memory, disk & network metrics
  • Service & process health monitoring
  • Event log monitoring & analysis
  • Hardware failure prediction

Automated Patch Management

Automated deployment of OS and third-party patches on a controlled schedule — tested, approved, and rolled out with rollback capability if anything goes wrong.

  • Windows, macOS & third-party patching
  • Scheduled maintenance windows
  • Patch compliance reporting
  • Rollback for failed updates

Automated Remediation

Pre-built and custom automation scripts resolve common issues instantly — restarting stuck services, clearing temp files, freeing disk space — without requiring human intervention or a helpdesk ticket.

  • Self-healing scripts for common failures
  • Automated disk cleanup & optimization
  • Service restart & recovery automation
  • Custom scripts for your environment

Endpoint Security & EDR Monitoring

Integrated monitoring of antivirus and EDR solutions — ensuring every endpoint has active protection, up-to-date definitions, and no unresolved threats lingering in your environment.

  • AV / EDR health & definition monitoring
  • Threat detection alert escalation
  • Endpoint isolation capability
  • Security posture scoring

Backup Monitoring & Verification

Every backup job is monitored and verified — failed or missed backups trigger immediate alerts, ensuring your data protection is never silently broken when you need it most.

  • Daily backup job status verification
  • Immediate alert on backup failures
  • Storage capacity monitoring
  • Restore test coordination

Asset & Inventory Management

Automated discovery and tracking of every hardware device and software installation in your environment — providing an always-current asset inventory for budgeting, audits, and lifecycle planning.

  • Automated hardware discovery & inventory
  • Software installation tracking
  • License compliance monitoring
  • Warranty & lifecycle tracking
24/7 Alert Response

We Never Sleep. Neither Does Our Monitoring.

Your business doesn't stop after 5pm — and neither do we. Our NOC team receives and investigates alerts around the clock, every day of the year. When an issue can't be resolved remotely, we coordinate on-site support to restore operations quickly.

Failed Backups

Immediate alert and investigation when any backup job fails — preventing silent data protection gaps.

Critical System Errors

Blue screens, kernel panics, application crashes, and critical event log entries trigger immediate NOC investigation and response.

Malware Detections

EDR and antivirus detections are escalated immediately — affected endpoints can be isolated within minutes to contain the threat.

Network & Service Outages

Connectivity failures, downed services, and WAN outages are detected and investigated in real time — with vendor escalation if needed.

Performance Degradation

CPU overloads, memory exhaustion, disk space thresholds, and slow response times are flagged before they impact end users.

Security Policy Violations

Disabled firewalls, missing AV, unauthorized software, or expired certificates are detected and remediated through automated or manual intervention.

Cloud Service Disruptions

Microsoft 365, Azure AD, and other cloud services are monitored for authentication failures, sync errors, and service degradation.

Hardware Health Warnings

Disk SMART failures, high temperatures, failed RAID arrays, and PSU warnings are caught early — long before they result in catastrophic hardware failure.
