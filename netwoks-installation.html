Core Services

Everything Included — Nothing Overlooked

Our network installations are fully scoped — covering every component from physical infrastructure to cloud connectivity, all delivered by certified engineers and backed by complete as-built documentation.

Structured Cabling

Professional Cat6/Cat6A and fiber cabling installed to TIA-568 standards — neatly routed, labeled, and terminated for maximum performance and long-term reliability.

  • Cat6 / Cat6A horizontal runs
  • Fiber optic backbone (single & multi-mode)
  • Rack, patch panel & cable management
  • ANSI/TIA-568 compliant labeling

Switching & VLAN Design

Core, distribution, and access layer switching with proper VLAN segmentation — isolating servers, workstations, VoIP, IoT, cameras, and guest traffic for performance and security.

  • Core / distribution / access layer design
  • VLAN segmentation & inter-VLAN routing
  • PoE / PoE+ for cameras, APs & phones
  • Spanning tree, port security & QoS

Firewall & VPN

Next-generation firewall deployment with IPS/IDS, application control, SSL inspection, and site-to-site or SSL VPN for secure remote access — from leading vendors including Fortinet, Palo Alto, and Cisco.

  • Next-gen firewall (NGFW) deployment
  • IPS / IDS, application & URL filtering
  • Site-to-site VPN & SSL VPN
  • Guest network isolation & NAC

Enterprise Wi-Fi Design

Business-class wireless deployments engineered with RF heat-mapping, proper AP placement, seamless roaming, and high-density design for open offices, warehouses, and multi-floor environments.

  • RF site survey & heat-map planning
  • Wi-Fi 6 / 6E access point deployment
  • Seamless roaming & band steering
  • Guest SSID with captive portal

SD-WAN & Internet Failover

Resilient WAN connectivity with automatic failover, SD-WAN path selection, and QoS policies that prioritize voice, video, and critical business applications across multiple ISP links.

  • Dual ISP / internet failover
  • SD-WAN intelligent path selection
  • QoS for VoIP, video & cloud apps
  • Multi-WAN load balancing

Monitoring & Network Baselines

After every installation, we configure network monitoring, establish performance baselines, and set up alerting — so you have immediate visibility into your infrastructure from day one.

  • SNMP / syslog monitoring setup
  • Bandwidth & latency baselines
  • Alert thresholds & notification routing
  • Integration with managed services NOC
Project Approach

From Discovery to Handover in 5 Steps

A structured, certified project process that delivers on time, on budget, and with zero surprises — from the first site visit to the final handover document.

01

Discovery

Requirements gathering, site conditions, constraints, existing infrastructure audit and user density analysis.

02

Design

Network topology, IP scheme, security architecture, Wi-Fi heat-map, and resiliency plan produced and reviewed.

03

Build

Cabling, hardware deployment, standardized configurations, VLAN setup, firewall rules, and Wi-Fi installation executed.

04

Validation

Performance tests, Wi-Fi coverage verification, failover testing, firewall rule validation, and acceptance test plan completed.

05

Handover

As-built diagrams, IP schema, config backups, runbooks, staff training, and ongoing support plan delivered.

Deliverables

What You Receive at Project Closeout

Every ECSG network installation closes with a comprehensive documentation package — ensuring your team can manage, troubleshoot, and expand your network long after the project ends.

Topology Diagrams

Full logical and physical network diagrams showing all devices, connections, VLANs, IP addressing, and WAN links — built as-installed and ready for future reference or expansion planning.

IP Addressing Plan

Complete IP schema documenting all subnets, VLAN IDs, DHCP ranges, static assignments, and gateway addresses — structured for easy future management, audits, and network growth.

Config Backups & Templates

Backed-up configurations for all switches, firewalls, routers, and APs — along with standardized templates so future changes can be made consistently and safely across your environment.

Wi-Fi Heat-Maps & Benchmarks

Post-installation RF heat-maps confirming coverage across your facility, with throughput benchmarks and channel utilization data — providing a baseline for ongoing performance monitoring.

Acceptance Test Report

Formal acceptance document confirming that all connectivity, VLAN routing, firewall policies, VPN tunnels, Wi-Fi coverage, and failover behavior have been tested and verified against specification.

Runbooks & SOPs

Step-by-step operational runbooks for common tasks: adding users, changing VLAN assignments, managing VPN access, performing firmware updates, and basic troubleshooting procedures.

Business Outcomes

What a Professional Network Delivers

A properly engineered network isn't just faster — it's more secure, easier to manage, and built to grow with your business without requiring a full redesign every few years.

Faster & More Stable

Properly sized switching, QoS policies, and optimized Wi-Fi eliminate the bottlenecks, dead zones, and congestion that slow your team down every day.

Reduced Security Risk

VLAN segmentation, next-gen firewalls, and guest isolation contain breaches and limit lateral movement — reducing your attack surface without slowing operations.

Built to Scale

A structured, documented network is easy to expand — add users, locations, or services without redesigning the foundation. Clear documentation means any engineer can pick up where we left off.

High Availability

Redundant links, automatic failover, and resilient WAN design keep your business connected even when individual components fail — minimizing downtime and its cost.
