Department 01 — Cybersecurity

Defense in depth: six layers, one coordinated response

This is the core of what Enterprise Cloud Solutions Group protects. No single layer is trusted to stop every threat alone — each one exists to catch what the layer before it missed, and all six report into a single monitoring function that never sleeps.

Each ring is a layer a threat must pass through. The monitoring function (below) watches all six at once, so a signal missed at one layer is caught by the correlation happening across the rest.

01

Identity & access protection

Multi-factor authentication, conditional access policies, risk-based sign-in checks, and privileged access controls make sure that only the right person, on the right device, under the right conditions, ever reaches a system.

02

Email threat defense

Every inbound and outbound message is screened for phishing, spoofed senders, business email compromise attempts, malicious links, and dangerous attachments before an employee ever sees them.

03

Endpoint detection & response

AI-driven monitoring runs on every laptop, desktop, and server, catching malicious behavior in real time, containing it automatically, and preserving the forensic detail needed to understand exactly what happened.

04

Secure browsing & DNS protection

Web traffic is filtered at the DNS level under a zero-trust model, blocking access to malicious or unauthorized destinations before a connection is ever made.

05

Perimeter & network security

Firewall-based services provide intrusion prevention, gateway antivirus scanning, geo-based traffic filtering, botnet blocking, sandboxed analysis of unknown files, and encrypted remote access for distributed teams.

06

24/7 monitoring & response

A dedicated security operations function correlates signals from every layer above around the clock, hunts for threats that individual tools miss on their own, and responds before an incident becomes a breach.

Department 02 — Infrastructure & Cloud

The platforms your team works on, every single day

Where the cybersecurity department protects, this department builds and runs. It designs, provisions, and maintains the cloud environments, productivity tools, and networks that carry the day-to-day work of your business.

Cloud infrastructure management

Provisioning, scaling, and maintaining virtual servers, storage, and networking resources in the cloud, sized to what the business actually needs and adjusted as it grows.

Productivity & collaboration platforms

Administration of business email, shared file storage, intranet spaces, and team collaboration tools, configured so people can find what they need and work together without friction.

Identity & directory administration

User provisioning, group and permission structures, and directory maintenance that keep access to every system organized as employees join, move, and leave.

Networking & connectivity

Design and upkeep of wired and wireless office networks, site-to-site links, and secure remote connectivity so every location and every remote employee stays connected.
