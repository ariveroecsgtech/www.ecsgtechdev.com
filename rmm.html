What Is RMM?

Proactive IT Care — Before Problems Happen

Remote Monitoring and Management (RMM) is the engine behind modern managed IT. A lightweight agent on every device sends continuous health data to ECSG's NOC — where automation and our engineers detect issues, apply fixes, and enforce security policies, often before your users ever notice a problem.

Proactive, Not Reactive

Traditional IT waits for things to break. ECSG's RMM flips that entirely. We watch every server, workstation, cloud service, and network device in real time — catching disk space issues, failing hardware, missed backups, and security policy violations before they become outages.

Our automated remediation scripts resolve common problems instantly — no ticket, no wait. When escalation is needed, our NOC team acts within minutes, resolving most issues remotely before your team is ever disrupted. Every plan includes real-time visibility, automated patching, and 24/7 alert response.
