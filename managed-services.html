What We Do

Complete IT Management, Done For You

Most small and mid-sized businesses can't afford — or don't need — a full in-house IT team. ECSG bridges that gap with a flat-rate, all-inclusive managed services model that gives you enterprise-grade support at a predictable monthly cost.

Proactive, Not Reactive

Traditional IT support waits for things to break. ECSG's managed services model is built around prevention — continuous monitoring, automated patching, real-time threat detection, and regular system health reviews keep your environment stable before issues arise.

When issues do occur, our helpdesk team is available around the clock. Your employees get fast, friendly support — by phone, email, or chat — with ticket tracking and SLA-backed response times.

Core Services

Everything Your Business Needs

Our managed services stack covers every layer of your IT — from the device on the desk to the server in the cloud — under a single, unified support model.

Remote Monitoring & Management

24/7 automated monitoring of all your devices, servers, and network. We detect and resolve issues before they impact your users.

  • Real-time alerts & automated remediation
  • Server, workstation & network monitoring
  • Performance & capacity reporting
  • SLA-backed uptime guarantee

Helpdesk & End-User Support

Your employees deserve fast, knowledgeable IT support. Our helpdesk handles Tier 1–3 issues so your team stays productive.

  • Phone, email & chat support channels
  • Ticketing system with full audit trail
  • Remote desktop support (instant access)
  • On-site dispatch when needed

Patch Management

Unpatched systems are the #1 cause of breaches. We automate OS and software updates across all endpoints — tested, scheduled, and documented.

  • Windows, macOS & third-party patching
  • Scheduled maintenance windows
  • Patch compliance reporting
  • Rollback capability for failed updates

Endpoint Security & EDR

Next-generation antivirus, Endpoint Detection & Response (EDR), and device policy enforcement — protecting every laptop, desktop, and server.

  • AI-driven threat detection (EDR/XDR)
  • Ransomware & malware protection
  • USB & removable media control
  • Threat isolation & automated response

Backup & Disaster Recovery

Comprehensive backup solutions for workstations, servers, and cloud workloads — with tested recovery procedures so you're never caught unprepared.

  • Automated daily backups (local + cloud)
  • Rapid restore (RTO < 4 hours)
  • Quarterly DR testing & documentation
  • Ransomware-proof immutable backups

Network Management

Your network is the foundation of your business. We design, monitor, and manage firewalls, switches, Wi-Fi, and connectivity for optimal performance and security.

  • Firewall management & rule optimization
  • Managed Wi-Fi & segmentation
  • VPN & secure remote access
  • Bandwidth monitoring & QoS

vCIO & Strategic Advisory

Your virtual CIO keeps your technology aligned with your business goals — planning investments, managing vendors, and delivering quarterly business reviews.

  • Quarterly Business Reviews (QBRs)
  • Technology roadmap & budget planning
  • Vendor negotiation & management
  • Risk assessment & compliance planning

Cybersecurity Management

Layered security that goes beyond antivirus — identity protection, email security, dark web monitoring, and compliance readiness for your industry.

  • Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
  • Dark web credential monitoring
  • Security awareness training
  • HIPAA, SOC 2 & CMMC readiness

Cloud & Microsoft 365 Management

Included in our managed services: complete administration of your Microsoft 365 and Azure environments, keeping them secure, compliant, and optimized.

  • M365 user & license management
  • Azure resource monitoring & cost control
  • Teams, SharePoint & OneDrive governance
  • Conditional Access & MFA policies
